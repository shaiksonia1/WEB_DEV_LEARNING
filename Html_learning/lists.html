Shaik Sonia Coffee recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon ground coffee (or coffee powder)
  • Milk (optional)
  • Sugar (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil water: Bring 1 cup of water to a boil in a pot or kettle.
  2. Add coffee: Once the water boils, add 1 tablespoon of ground coffee (adjust to your taste)
  3. Brew: Let the coffee brew for 3-5 minutes for stronger flavor.
  4. Strain or stir: If using ground coffee, strain it using a coffee filter or strainer. If using instant coffee powder, stir well.
  5. Add milk and sugar (optional): Add milk and sugar to taste, depending on your preference.
  6. Serve: Pour the coffee into a cup and enjoy!
